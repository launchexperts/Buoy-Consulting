Why we stay deliberately small.

Most consultancies grow by adding juniors and reselling them at senior rates. We grow by adding seniors and selling them at senior rates — or not adding anyone at all. Six is enough to cover any engagement we'd take on, and small enough that you'll always know exactly who's doing the work.

Every consultant on the team has at least fifteen years of construction or civil finance experience. We don't hire generalists and put them through a four-week Jobpac course. The bar to join Buoy is the bar that makes you valuable to a client on day one.
