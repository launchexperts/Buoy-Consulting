Support — Payroll

Construction payroll done right — awards, EBAs, and STP.

Specialist Jobpac payroll support for construction and civil businesses: award- and EBA-compliant pays, super, STP, leave, and timesheet integration.

What we cover.

Construction payroll is unforgiving. Get the EBA wrong and you're underpaying. Get the timesheet allocation wrong and your project costs are wrong too. We run construction payroll inside Jobpac with the rates, allowances, and on-costs configured the way your award or EBA actually requires — with the audit trail to back it up.

  • Weekly & fortnightly cyclesRun end-to-end on schedule, with sign-off and bank file ready for upload.
  • Award & EBA compliancePay rates, penalty rates, allowances, RDOs — configured correctly and reviewed when the award/EBA is updated.
  • SuperannuationContributions calculated, batched, and remitted on time.
  • Single Touch Payroll (STP)Each pay reported to the ATO under STP Phase 2 with the correct categorisations.
  • Leave administrationAnnual, personal, long-service, RDO accruals and balances reconciled monthly.
  • Timesheet integrationTimesheets imported from your collection method, allocated to the right projects and cost codes.

Why construction payroll trips most generalist providers.

  • EBAs vary by employer and by site — one rate sheet doesn't cover everyone.
  • Allowances (site, travel, fares, meal) need to flow through to project cost, not just to the pay slip.
  • Working time directives, RDOs, and rolling rosters complicate accruals.
  • Subbie/labour-hire mixed workforces need clear separation in the GL and the project ledger.
