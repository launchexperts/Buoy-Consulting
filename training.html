Training

Hands‑on Jobpac training tailored to your team.

Generic Jobpac training videos are easy to find. Training tailored to your modules, your project structure, and your team's actual experience level isn't. We deliver training that's grounded in your real Jobpac environment — not a clean demo system that looks nothing like yours.

Built for your team, not the textbook.

Every training engagement starts with a 30-minute call to understand who's in the room. New starters who've never seen Jobpac? Long-timers picking up new modules? PMs who only need to read reports?

We design the syllabus from there. Sessions run on a sandbox of your real Jobpac environment, with your project structure and cost codes — so what people learn is what they'll actually do on Monday.

Formats we run.

  • On-site workshopsHalf-day or full-day sessions at your office, with a senior consultant in the room.
  • Remote sessionsLive, interactive, screen-shared. Recorded so the team can rewatch.
  • Role-specific tracksProject managers, accounts payable, payroll, finance leadership — each gets training calibrated to what they touch.
  • New-starter packsA fixed onboarding programme so the next hire doesn't take three months to come up to speed.
  • Recorded libraryWe'll record the sessions and hand you a private library your team can reference long after we leave.
