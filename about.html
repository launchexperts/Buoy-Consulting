About Buoy

Specialist Jobpac consultants — built by people who've actually run construction businesses.

Buoy Consulting was founded in 2010 by senior finance and construction operators who'd spent decades inside the industry. Sixteen years on, we're still the only consultancy in Australia and New Zealand focused exclusively on Jobpac for construction and civil businesses.

Why we exist.

Most Jobpac consultancies are reseller-aligned generalists who'll touch any ERP. We're the opposite. Every consultant on our team has spent at least fifteen years inside a construction or civil business doing the work — running month-end, signing off subbie claims, dealing with a CFO who needs the forecast in the morning.

That's not a bio line. It's the reason our advice lands instead of bouncing off. When you tell us your retentions are out by $80k against your subbies' books, we don't ask you to explain what a retention is.

How we're different

What you actually get when you hire Buoy.

01 / Same-day support

Answered the same day, every day.

Not a 48-hour SLA. Not "next business day." Most tickets are answered within hours by a senior who can actually solve them. When month-end is tomorrow, that's the difference that matters.

02 / No juniors

The senior who pitched does the work.

Most consultancies sell you the partner and deliver you a graduate. We don't have graduates. The person who scoped your engagement is the person sitting at the keyboard.

03 / Vendor-independent

We don't sell licences.

We don't earn commission from your Jobpac subscription. We earn fees from making your existing investment deliver. That changes whose side we're on.

04 / Construction-native

Civil, commercial, and building.

We've worked across Tier-2 builders, civil contractors, fit-out specialists, and family-owned regional businesses. We know what stage claims, retentions, and plant cost recovery look like in Jobpac because we've configured them ourselves.

05 / Fixed scope, no surprises

Priced before we start.

Every engagement starts with a fixed-scope proposal. You see the price, the timeline, and the deliverables before you sign anything. We don't run the meter.

06 / Embedded knowledge

Your team owns it when we leave.

Documentation, training, and a real handover. We're consultants — not a permanent dependency. The goal is for your team to run Jobpac without us.

Want to know if we're the right fit?

A 30-minute call, no slide deck, no pressure. We'll tell you whether we can help — and if we can't, who would.
